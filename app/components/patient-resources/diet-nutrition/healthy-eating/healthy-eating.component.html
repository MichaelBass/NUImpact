Importance of Healthy Eating

Listen to the full audio of this page below

Much research has been done to determine the effect of diet on health. Overall, here are some main benefits of healthy eating for patients like you.

Benefits are:

  • Reduce the risk of a second cancer diagnosis.
  • Reduce your weight, which will also protect you against cancer and other health conditions such as heart disease, diabetes and stroke.
  • Bring back energy from treatment and may help you to feel better throughout the day.
  • You feel better in your body!
