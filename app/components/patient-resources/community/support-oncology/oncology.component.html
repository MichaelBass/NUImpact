Supportive Oncology at Northwestern Medicines

Listen to the full audio of this page below

Supportive oncology services complement your cancer treatment plan by providing you with emotional and practical support throughout your entire cancer journey.

At Northwestern Medicine, supportive oncology outpatient services include:

  • Counseling delivered by board-certified social workers and clinical counselors
  • Pain and symptom management (also known as palliative care)
  • Personalized cancer nutrition counseling
  • Educational classes, support groups, and referrals to community resources
